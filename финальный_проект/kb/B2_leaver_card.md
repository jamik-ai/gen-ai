# Category B: deactivating a card on leaver (employee departure)

HR must submit the leaver form at least **3 business days** before the employee's last working day. The
access card is automatically blocked on the date specified in the form.

The HR Business Partner is responsible for submitting the form on time. If the form is submitted late, the
card is blocked manually on request to the Security Desk, with a manual-block SLA of **2 hours**.
